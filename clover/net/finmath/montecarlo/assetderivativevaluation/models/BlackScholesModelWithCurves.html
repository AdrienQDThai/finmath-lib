15    /**
16    * This class implements a Black Scholes Model, that is, it provides the drift and volatility specification
17    * and performs the calculation of the numeraire (consistent with the dynamics, i.e. the drift).
18    *
19    * The model is
20    * \[
21    * dS = r S dt + \sigma S dW, \quad S(0) = S_{0},
22    * \]
23    * \[
24    * dN = r N dt, \quad N(0) = N_{0},
25    * \]
26    *
27    * The class provides the model of S to an <code>{@link net.finmath.montecarlo.process.MonteCarloProcess}</code> via the specification of
28    * \( f = exp \), \( \mu = r - \frac{1}{2} \sigma^2 \), \( \lambda_{1,1} = \sigma \), i.e.,
29    * of the SDE
30    * \[
31    * dX = \mu dt + \lambda_{1,1} dW, \quad X(0) = \log(S_{0}),
32    * \]
33    * with \( S = f(X) \). See {@link net.finmath.montecarlo.process.MonteCarloProcess} for the notation.
34    *
35    * @author Christian Fries
